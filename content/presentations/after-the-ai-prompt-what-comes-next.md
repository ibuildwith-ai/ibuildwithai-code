[Ascend GenAI](https://luma.com/ascendgenai) has put together a great event. See details below!

AI is evolving quickly, but most conversations are still focused on prompts, tools, and surface-level use cases.

This event is about what comes next.

We are bringing together founders, engineers, and builders who are working directly with generative AI in real-world settings from building companies to designing systems to deploying models.

Instead of focusing on what AI could do, this event focuses on what is actually happening now.

### Key Topics Covered

- How AI is shaping companies and industries
- How AI systems and workflows are being built
- How models are applied in real-world environments
