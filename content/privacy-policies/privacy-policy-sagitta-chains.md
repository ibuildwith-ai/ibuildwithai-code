### The short version

**Sagitta Chains does not collect your personal data.** The game has no user accounts, no ads, no third-party analytics, and no tracking. Everything the game saves (your scores, stats, settings, and a randomly generated identifier) stays **on your device** and is never transmitted to us or anyone else. This policy applies to Sagitta Chains on both iOS and Android.

### What the app stores (on your device only)

To make the game work and remember your progress, Sagitta Chains stores the following **locally on your device**, using your device's standard app storage:

- Your best scores and highest level reached, per difficulty
- Lifetime play statistics (e.g., levels cleared, longest chain)
- Your settings (sound on/off, haptics on/off)
- Your power-up inventory
- A randomly generated identifier that exists only on your device, used to keep your local records together

This information **never leaves your device**. We cannot see it, and it is not sent to any server (there is no server). If you delete the app, this data is removed with it. You can also clear it any time with **Reset Progress** in the in-app Settings.

### What we do **not** collect

- No name, email, phone number, or contact information
- No precise or approximate location
- No advertising identifiers; no advertising
- No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs
- No social, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access

### Crash and performance diagnostics

Sagitta Chains does not include any third-party crash-reporting tool. If your device settings allow it, Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) may share **aggregated, anonymized** crash and performance diagnostics with us through the platform's own tools (Apple's "Share With App Developers" setting / Xcode Organizer; Google Play "Android vitals"). This is controlled by **your** device settings and governed by Apple's and Google's privacy policies, not ours. These reports do not identify you.

### Children's privacy

Sagitta Chains does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, the app does not knowingly gather information from children under 13 (or the equivalent age in your region).

### Your choices

- **Reset Progress** (Settings) erases all locally stored game data.
- **Deleting the app** removes all locally stored game data.
- Sound and haptics can be turned off in Settings.

### Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the effective date and post the revised version at this URL. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
