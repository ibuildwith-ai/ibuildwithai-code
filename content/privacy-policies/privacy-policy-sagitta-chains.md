### The short version

**Sagitta Chains does not collect your personal data.** The game has no user accounts, no ads, no third-party analytics, and no tracking. Everything the game saves (your scores, stats, settings, purchases, and a randomly generated identifier) stays **on your device**, with one optional exception: if you turn on iCloud sync, your best scores sync through **your own private Apple iCloud account**, which we cannot access. Nothing is ever transmitted to us. This policy applies to Sagitta Chains on iOS.

### What the app stores (on your device only)

To make the game work and remember your progress, Sagitta Chains stores the following **locally on your device**, using your device's standard app storage:

- Your best scores and highest level reached
- Lifetime play statistics (e.g., levels cleared, longest chain)
- Your settings (sound, haptics, theme, and similar preferences)
- Which supporter purchase you own, if any, so your supporter badge and themes stay unlocked
- A randomly generated identifier that exists only on your device, used to keep your local records together

Except for the optional iCloud sync described below, this information **never leaves your device**, and we cannot see it. If you delete the app, this local data is removed with it. You can also clear it any time with **Reset Progress** in the in-app Settings.

### iCloud sync (optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your best scores (high score, highest level, longest chain, and most chains in a level) sync across your Apple devices through **your own private iCloud account**, using Apple's iCloud (CloudKit). This data lives in your personal iCloud space; **we cannot read it, and it is never sent to a server of ours.** iCloud sync is optional: you can turn it on or off, sync on demand, or delete your synced scores at any time from the in-app Settings. Your use of iCloud is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

### In-app purchases

Sagitta Chains offers optional purchases (a "Supporter" and "Supporter+" tip that unlock cosmetic extras such as a badge and themes). **All purchases are processed by Apple.** We never receive your payment card or billing information. The app only records **which tier you own**, stored on your device (and tied to your Apple ID so you can restore purchases on your other devices). We do not collect any personal or financial data through purchases.

### What we do **not** collect

- No name, email, phone number, or contact information
- No precise or approximate location
- No advertising identifiers; no advertising
- No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs
- No payment or financial information (purchases are handled entirely by Apple)
- No social, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access

### Crash and performance diagnostics

Sagitta Chains does not include any third-party crash-reporting tool. If your device settings allow it, Apple may share **aggregated, anonymized** crash and performance diagnostics with us through the App Store's own tools (Apple's "Share With App Developers" setting / Xcode Organizer). This is controlled by **your** device settings and governed by Apple's privacy policy, not ours. These reports do not identify you.

### Children's privacy

Sagitta Chains does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, the app does not knowingly gather information from children under 13 (or the equivalent age in your region).

### Your choices

- **Reset Progress** (Settings) erases all locally stored game data.
- **iCloud Sync** (Settings) can be turned on or off, and you can delete your synced scores at any time.
- **Deleting the app** removes all locally stored game data.
- Sound and haptics can be turned off in Settings.

### Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the effective date and post the revised version at this URL. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
