Software creation is changing fast. AI now gives knowledge workers and domain experts a new kind of leverage. If you understand your users, your workflows, and your business constraints, you can turn that understanding into prototypes, tools, and automations by collaborating with AI. The shift is not that everyone must become a developer. The shift is that domain expertise plus AI is becoming the new baseline.

I have spent over 30 years building and shipping products across multiple technology shifts, including the computer, internet, and mobile eras. I have founded several startups and worked across a wide range of roles at companies including Cigna, Toyota, NBC, J.F. Shea, and Walt Disney Imagineering. Having seen how new platforms consistently change who gets to build and how ideas move from concept to reality, I created iBuildWith.ai to help domain experts apply their expertise with AI in a structured way, so what they build stays useful, understandable, and easier to evolve over time.

My domain expertise is content architecture, content modeling, headless CMS, and product development. I'm a certified Contentful Manager and Certified Contentful Professional, and I founded the Headless Creator community, which was later acquired by Uniform.dev.

Today, at Cigna, as a Principal Agentic Builder, I combine my domain expertise with AI to create agentic workflows, prototypes, tools, and proofs of concept that help enterprise teams move from ideas to working systems.
